Cosmo Squabble

ID

July 2018
Internship Project

Duration : 1 month
Tools : Unity

Roles

Game Design
Playtest planning
Balancing

Cosmo Squabble is a 2 versus 2 shooter which lets the players embody alien pilots and their irreplaceable AI co-pilots in a comical spaceship battle, their goal is to destroy the opponents' ship.


The most important aspect of the game is the cooperation dynamic for each duo : The pilot controls the ship with his pawn, whereas the co-pilot triggers various attacks and effects with his deck of connected cards. This complementarity implies communication and coordination, but also allows intergenerational play. Indeed, people with different skills and gaming background can share the same experience because each role demands different skills or attitude of the player.


As an example, our playtests showed that a parent with absolutely no gaming experience can play in cooperation with its more experienced child by leaving them the "attack and dodge" pilot role and playing the more strategic co-pilot position.

What I learned

Through this project I learned a lot by designing an experience on Wizama’s unique platform. Indeed the physical interaction allowed by their connected cards and pawns with direction detection was both a challenge and an interesting aspect put at the center of each game's experience.
