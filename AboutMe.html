About Me

Hi ! I am a French game programmer living in Stockholm. I graduated from a bachelor in Game Design and a master in Game Programming in France and I have now been living in Sweden for more than a year.

Discovering things is what I love the most, from learning new techniques to exploring intriguing concepts. I try to be curious, openminded and reliable in my work, and I hope that my journey will lead to a lot of growth and lived experiences.
Of course I also enjoy meeting new people and I believe in building safe spaces, meaningful projects and healthy dynamics.
