Wrecking Paris

ID

April 2018
School Project

Duration : 1 week
Tools : Unity

Roles

Programming
Game Design

Wrecking Paris is a mobile game challenging the player on their combination skills and reflexes against waves of different enemies. Combining different basic attacks (elements on the right) creates new attacks (on the bottom) defined by their power, range and element (adding a side effect). The player must chose wisely and also tap on the screen to destroy missiles coming at them.

What I learned

This prototype is my very first developping experience for mobile, therefore I learned how to use a tactile interface and keep the game design coherent with both its strength and constraints.
Furthermore, our combination system and enemies allowed me to practice and structure the use of inheritance and Unity's ScriptableObjects.
