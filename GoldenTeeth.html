Golden Teeth is a fast paced twin-stick shooter prototype.
As a pirate travelling in a world of flying chunks of islands, the player must defeat the Captain and his crew in order to claim his treasure.
Due to this disjointed envrionment, the player and his enemies can only move from spot to spot thanks to swift jumps.

What I learned

Through this first project on Unity, I assimilated the basics of programming on Unity 2D(enemy behaviour, player controller) and asset integration.
We worked on this project for 5 days with a team of 6 sutdents (game designers and artists), hence I learned how to manage a short term project with a small team.
