Golden Teeth

Duration : 5 days
Tools : Unity (2D)

Roles

Project Leading
Programming
Game Design

Golden Teeth is a fast paced twin-stick shooter prototype.
As a pirate traveling in a world of flying chunks of islands, the player must defeat the Captain and his crew in order to claim his treasure.
Due to this disjointed envrionment, the player and his enemies can only move from spots to spots thanks to swift jumps.



This prototype was developed in 5 days and was our first project using the Unity Engine.
