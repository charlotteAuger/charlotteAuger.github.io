About Me

Hi ! Currently studying in Supinfogame Rubika in France, I'm in my M2 in Game Programming and Management after a Bachelor in Game Design. I'm gameplay oriented and my first experiences with AI programming inspire me to progress in that field.
My strong interest for game design also has a great influence on the way I work and interact with the other members of a team, and I am always keen to discover and learn new techniques.

This portoflio is a selection of the projects I found the most interesting and learned the most from, feel free to contact me if you have any question !
