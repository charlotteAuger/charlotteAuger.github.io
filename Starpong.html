Starpong VR is an arcade table tennis game where players must destroy the opponents solar system.
Our goal is to create a dynamic experience through accessible gameplay, fun powers and powerful smashes.

Available soon

What I learned

As gameplay programmer on this project, I learned a lot about VR problematics in terms of controller and the use of the VR Toolkit for Unity.
The online nature of the game also led me to practice reasoning for network development (replication, simulating physic to compensate lag and so on...).
Furthermore, I worked on a generic power system, allowing an easy addition of new powers during the development of the game.
