As Artémidore, the player embodies a small crab in the search of his disappeared friend and parental figure : a gigantic Hermit Crab. This journey leads him inside the empty shell of his friend where he discovers a confusing oniric world representing his travel through loss and self reconstruction.


This adventure game has the particularity to feature a world without up or down, therefore the player can only move by walking on surfaces, regardless of it's orientation. They can only leave a surface for another by diving in a water current and grabbing the next surface.


Artémidores other skills can be unlocked and combined by the player in ordre to choose their favorite playstyle and thus represent the self reconstruction of the protagonist. Examples of skills are the possibility of grabbing seaweed to hide it's shell from predatorso or having an extensible pincer allowing to grab things further. Combining those skills grants a better access to camouflage.

Our narrative bible

What I learned

The developpement of this prototype led to work on an atypical 3rd person controller allowing the player to walk on any surface regardless of common gravity, which was both a technical and a design challenge. I also worked on an enemy AI using a first iteration on adapting the A* pathfinding algorithm in a 3 dimensionnal space in order to emulate swimming.
