A Planet in the Fog is a First Person Exploration game centered on scientific research and understanding the ecosystem of a planet covered in fog.
This graduation project is currently in pre-production until January 2020.

My work

During the pre-prodution I was in charge of finding technical solutions for our main game problematics.


Fog : The fog being a central element to the game, I searched for technologies that would allow our artists to achieve something more realistic. I settled on the Nvidia Flow technology after some research, found source code including the technology and compiled it for our team to work with.


Pheromone diffusion : Our ecosystem is tightly linked to pheromones that are present in the fog and that follow wind currents on the surface of the planet. I prototyped a grid system to simulate the diffusion of those pheromones and worked on a basic tool in order to let the level designer build the wind currents easily.


Ecosystems AIs : Goal Oriented Action Planning seems to be the most suitable technic to manage individual agents interacting in our ecosystem. I worked on adapting a GOAP framework to our needs : first by making the functionnalities of the framework accessible for our game designers in the engine's editor, and secondly to make it compatible with our pheromone diffusion system.
