In Ostora, the player takes the control of Adil, a young man waking up in a mechanical body centuries after his era.
This action/adventure game demo leads the player to discover a world with arabic inspirations built around a mystical religion and the ruins of a highly technological past.
He must understand and master this technology and its mysterious Energy in order to discover what happened to him.


Developed with a team of 5 game designers and 3 game artists, this project was our first work of this scale. It features a narrative area, an exploration zone, a dungeon, a boss and 4 gameplay features.

What I learned

This project being our first video game project of this scale, I learned a lot about the game development workflow and working in a bigger team. Assuming the programming of the game was very instructive regarding gameplay programming (game mechanics, enemy behaviour, level design interactions) but also in terms of communication with the other actors in the production (artists, sound designer, level designer . . .).
