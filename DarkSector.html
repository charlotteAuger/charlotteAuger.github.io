This boardgame was realized as a school project, during 8 months we worked as a team of 2 game designers and 2 artists in order to create a fully finished product.


Dark Sector's adventure takes place in a science-fiction world where humanity has expanded through space. 2 to 4 players cooperate as an expedition team sent to a sector devastated by an unknown alien threat. Their mission is to learn more about this threat to prepare the Ultralliance for the oncoming invasion.


The game focuses on :

Exploration : The Cards composing the different places and the Event Booklet allow each exploration to be unique.

Survival : Players are pressured by limited oxygene and dangerous encounters with aliens.

Deduction : A set of caracteristics for the alien threat is drawn at the begining of each game with hints the player has to unlock.
